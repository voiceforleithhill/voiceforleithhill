Data Protection Statement

We take the protection of your data very seriously. It is kept in a secure database under the control of our Data Protection Officers. It is used solely to keep you informed about the activities of 'A Voice for Leith Hill', to invite you to events and gatherings and occasionally to ask you to help our fund-raising efforts. If you wish to unsubscribe to our database, view the information we hold about you or change that information, please contact us through the website and we will answer your request as soon as possible.
